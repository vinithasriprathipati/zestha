Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 2010, Zestha has been serving authentic Italian cuisine with a passion for quality and tradition. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, bringing the true flavors of Italy to Mumbai.

Every dish is crafted with love, using only the finest imported ingredients and locally sourced fresh produce. Our chefs combine traditional techniques with modern culinary artistry to create unforgettable dining experiences.

Signature Dishes

Meet Our Head Chef

Marco Rossini

With over 20 years of culinary experience across Italy and India, Chef Marco brings authentic Italian flavors to every plate. Trained in Rome and Milan, he specializes in traditional pasta-making and wood-fired pizza.

"Cooking is an art, and every dish tells a story. At Zestha, we pour our hearts into creating memories for our guests." - Chef Marco
